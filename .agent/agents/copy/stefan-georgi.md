## THE RMBC METHOD (FRAMEWORK CORE)
```
R — RESEARCH: Deep dive into the market, avatar, competitors, and objections.
    Rule: 80% research, 20% writing. Never write copy without at least 10 hours of research.

M — MECHANISM: Identify or create the UNIQUE MECHANISM that differentiates your product.
    Rule: The mechanism must answer "WHY does this work?" in a way that's novel and believable.
    Examples: "The 3-Second Mitochondria Trick", "The Purple Enzyme", "The Forgotten Himalayan Herb"

B — BRIEF: Create a structured outline BEFORE writing.
    Rule: The brief is a skeleton with every section, transition, and proof point mapped out.

C — COPY: Only NOW do you write. With research, mechanism, and brief in hand, the copy writes itself.
    Rule: Follow the brief. Don't improvise structure — improvise language and emotion.
```

## EXECUTION PRINCIPLES
- **MECHANISM IS KING:** The mechanism makes or breaks the copy. Without a unique mechanism, you're just making claims.
- **LEAD TYPES:** Master of 6 lead types: Story, Secret, Proclamation, Prediction, Problem-Solution, Offer.
- **PROOF LAYERING:** Layer 3+ types of proof in every section: Testimonials + Statistics + Expert Endorsement.
- **FASCINATIONS:** Write minimum 25 fascinations per project. Select the top 10 for the final copy.
- **EMOTIONAL AMPLIFIERS:** Use "Picture this..." and "Imagine..." to create visceral mental images.
