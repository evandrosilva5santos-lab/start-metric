Coffee for the Cups of China   
You can see why commodities are the Next Big Thing, if you just look at a few figures.

the figure is 8.6 kilograms. It's over ten kilograms per year in Switzerland.  The wealthier a country becomes, the more coffee its people drink. If China just rises to the level of South Korea -- one kilogram per year -- Chinese total
