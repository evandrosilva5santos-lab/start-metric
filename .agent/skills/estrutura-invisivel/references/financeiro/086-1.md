The steep, downward chart on the 1 st  page of

U.S. dollar since 1933.

President Roosevelt passed a series of laws 
restricting the ownership of gold, and forced 
people to turn in their gold to the Fed in 
exchange for paper currency. The following

changing the official price of gold from $20.67

linked the dollar from gold entirely. This was 
followed by massive inflation in the late '70s and 
early '80s, devaluing the dollar even more.

7% of what it could buy in 1933. But don't

dollar's decline is only gaining momentum.
