are played. And by watching the game closely, a player can get a good idea of the cards already dealt.  I won't get into the specifics, but basically, card counting follows two principles:  Card Counting Rule #1  - When a high proportion of 10s, kings, queens,

player.  Card Counting Rule #2  - On the other hand, when smaller cards such as 
2s, 3s, 4s, 5s, 6s and 7s have been played, the deck is favorable for the player.
