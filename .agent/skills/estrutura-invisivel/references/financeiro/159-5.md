No Belize taxes   
     If you qualify for Belize's QRP retiree program, you don't pay Belize taxes on income earned outside the country.       Specifically, under the law, you're exempt from:       "the payment of all taxes and levies on all income or receipts that accrue [to you] from a service outside of Belize...whether such income is earned or passive income and whether or not such income is remitted [to you] in Belize."       Furthermore, as a qualified participant in the country's QRP program for retirees, you can bring your car (even your jeep, boat, van, or small airplane) and all your personal and household effects into Belize with you...completely tax-free.
