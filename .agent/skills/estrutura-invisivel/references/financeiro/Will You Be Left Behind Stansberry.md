Gresham's LawAnd one of the most powerful forces of economics and human
behavior proves this fact, over thousands of years...
Gresham’s Law explains what happens, throughout history, when
there are two or more different types of money (of different values)
in an economy — like the dollar and Bitcoin today.
Gresham’s Law says that inevitably, people spend the “bad”
money, of lesser value... and hoard the “good” money (as savings),
because it will be worth much more in the future.
