1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Line 2 baking  sheets with foil.  
2. In small bowl, sift together cocoa, salt,  and 1⁄4 cup of the sugar.
3. In large bowl, with electric mixer at medium-low  speed,  beat  egg 
whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks  form.  Beat in remaining  
1⁄4 cup sugar, 1⁄2 tablespoon at a time,  until meringue  is glossy  and stiff 
peaks form. Fold in cocoa mixture and  vanilla  extract.
4. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls about  1˝ apart  onto  prepared  baking  
sheets. Bake 25 minutes for soft chewy cookies  or 40 minutes  for 
crisp ones. Dust cooled cookies with confectioners’  sugar.
