Channel Builder is a powerful networking 
tool that allows Independent Software 
Vendors, System Builders, Resellers and 
other Microsoft Gold and Certified Partners  
to easily connect, collaborate, solve 
problems and increase revenue.  
 
 
Say you’ve developed a great Solution 
built on the Microsoft platform.  
 
Using Channel Buil der you can post your 
solution, specifying the industry it serves, 
the specific problem it solves, the cost and 
a staggering array of other details.  
 
 
Once you’ve posted, partners from 
Mumbai to Maryland can search the 
Channel Builder database, specify jus t the 
sort of capabilities they’re looking for and 
connect with you to bring your solution to 
the people that need it.  
 
Posting and searching for Opportunities is 
just as easy, and just as powerful.  
 
 
Ready to expand your business and connect 
with talented , experienced Microsoft Gold 
and Certified Partners around the world?  
 
Signing up is just the click of a mouse 
away.
