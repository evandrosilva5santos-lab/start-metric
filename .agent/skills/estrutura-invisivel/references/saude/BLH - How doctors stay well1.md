If your blood pressure is high, chances are your
doctor will tell you to watch your sodium intake.Unfortunately, that advice is not always correct.
You see, it’s not sodium intake per se that aggra-

ness, abdominal bloating, and even heart disease.

ratio is to get 5 times as much potassium as sodium in your diet. Un-
fortunately, the typical American diet is backward — providing twice
as much sodium as potassium!
